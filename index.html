ABOUT

The Ithaca Micromobility Initiative is a collaborative project aimed at integrating electric bikes onto Cornell University’s campus through a partnership with Ithaca Bikeshare. While Cornell has currently declined to formally partner with the program, the initiative continues to build momentum by deploying a Micromobility Insights Dashboard to highlight the tangible benefits of bikeshare systems for the Cornell community.

With data provided by the Director of Micromobility at the Center for Community Transportation, early findings reveal that approximately 75% of bikeshare trips begin or end within Cornell’s campus, underscoring high student engagement and need. Long‑term goals include securing funding through New York State grants and local foundations to support sustainable transportation, reduce reliance on private vehicles, and contribute to campus‑wide emissions reductions.
